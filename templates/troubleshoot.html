Quick Reference

General Security First-Aid

01

Disconnect

For active infections, immediately disconnect from the internet to prevent data exfiltration and spread.

02

Document

Screenshot and record all symptoms, error messages, and unusual behavior for forensic analysis.

03

Scan

Run a full system scan with your antivirus and a second-opinion tool like Malwarebytes.

04

Change Credentials

After remediation, change all passwords and enable MFA on all critical accounts.

05

Report

Report incidents to your IT team, relevant authorities, and affected platforms to prevent spread.

06

Restore

After cleaning the system, restore from clean backups if any data was compromised or encrypted.
